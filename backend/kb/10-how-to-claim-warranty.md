# How to Claim Warranty

If your product develops a fault covered under warranty (see "Warranty Coverage" for what qualifies), here's how to claim it.

**Steps:**
1. Locate your Order ID or the warranty card that came with your product
2. Contact GrowMart support and describe the issue in detail — include when the problem started and any troubleshooting you've already tried
3. Support may ask you to try a few basic troubleshooting steps first (reset, firmware update, reseating a connection) to rule out a non-hardware issue
4. If the issue persists, support will approve a warranty claim and offer one of:
   - A replacement unit shipped free of charge (most common for devices under 6 months old)
   - A repair through our authorized service center (typical turnaround: 7–10 business days)
   - A refund, in rare cases where the product model is discontinued or out of stock for replacement

**What you'll need to provide:** Order ID, a description of the fault, and in most cases a short video or photo showing the issue (e.g., device not powering on, error light pattern).

**Turnaround time:** Warranty claims are typically resolved within 3–5 business days for replacements, or 7–10 business days if a physical repair is needed.

**Out-of-warranty products:** If your product is outside its warranty period, our service center can still often repair it at a paid, discounted rate — contact support for a quote.
