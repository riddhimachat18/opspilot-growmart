# Delayed or Lost Order

We know a delayed order is frustrating, especially when you were counting on it. Here's what to do.

**If your order is delayed (tracking shows movement but past the estimated window):**
- Wait until 2 business days past the estimated delivery date, since minor courier delays are common
- Check the tracking link for the latest status and any exception notes (e.g., "address issue," "customer not available")
- If no resolution after 2 extra business days, contact support with your Order ID for an investigation

**If your order appears lost (no tracking movement for 5+ business days, or status stuck at "Dispatched"):**
- Contact GrowMart support with your Order ID
- We will raise a formal investigation with the courier partner, which typically takes 3–5 business days to resolve
- If the courier partner confirms the package is lost in transit, GrowMart will offer a full refund or a free replacement, whichever you prefer

**If tracking shows "Delivered" but you did not receive the package:**
- Check with neighbors, building security, or other household members first
- If still not located within 24 hours, contact support immediately — delivery disputes must be reported within 48 hours of the "Delivered" status for us to investigate with the courier partner

Support will need your Order ID, registered phone number, and a brief description of the issue to begin an investigation.
