# Contacting Support and Escalation Process

**Ways to reach GrowMart support:**
- In-app/website chat (fastest for most queries, available 9 AM–9 PM IST daily)
- Email: support@growmart.example (response within 24 hours)
- Phone: available for GrowMart Plus members via priority line, and for urgent issues (damaged items, payment disputes) for all customers

**What to have ready:** Your Order ID speeds up resolution significantly for any order-related query. For account issues, your registered email or phone number is helpful.

**Escalation to a human agent:** Our AI support assistant handles most common queries (order status, returns, troubleshooting, account issues) instantly. It will automatically escalate to a human support agent when:
- The issue requires a refund above ₹500, per our fraud-prevention review process
- The same issue has recurred multiple times and remains unresolved
- You explicitly request to speak with a human agent
- The query falls outside standard policy and needs case-by-case judgment

**Callback requests:** If escalated, you can choose to schedule a callback at a convenient time rather than waiting in a live queue — available slots are shown based on current support team availability, typically within 24 hours.

**Complaint escalation beyond first-line support:** If you're unsatisfied with a resolution, you can request escalation to a senior support lead by replying "escalate" in any support conversation, or emailing escalations@growmart.example directly with your Order ID and prior conversation reference.
