# Bulk and Corporate Orders

GrowMart supports bulk purchases for corporate gifting, events, and reselling inquiries, separate from our standard retail pricing.

**Eligibility:** Orders of 10 or more units of the same product, or mixed-product orders with a total value above ₹15,000, qualify for bulk pricing consideration.

**Bulk pricing tiers (indicative, subject to product and final quote):**
- 10–24 units: 8% off listed retail price
- 25–49 units: 12% off listed retail price
- 50+ units: 15–18% off listed retail price, with custom quote

**How to request a bulk order:**
1. Contact GrowMart support with the product(s), quantity, and delivery timeline needed
2. A bulk order specialist will share a formal quote within 1–2 business days, including any applicable GST invoicing details for business buyers
3. Bulk orders typically require advance payment (50% advance, 50% on dispatch for large orders) and have separate delivery timelines depending on stock availability

**Corporate gifting customization:** Options like custom packaging or branded inserts may be available for large orders (50+ units) — mention this when requesting a quote, as it affects both pricing and lead time.

**Note:** Bulk order inquiries are handled by a separate specialist queue and are not eligible for standard consumer return/refund timelines — bulk order terms are confirmed in the formal quote before payment.
