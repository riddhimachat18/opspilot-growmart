# LED Strip Light Installation and Troubleshooting

**Installation steps:**
1. Clean the surface where you'll stick the LED strip with rubbing alcohol and let it dry fully — adhesive won't stick well to dusty or oily surfaces
2. Peel the adhesive backing in small sections as you apply, pressing firmly along the length
3. Connect the strip to the included power adapter and plug into a nearby outlet
4. Pair with the GrowMart Home app: "Add Device" → "LED Strip" → follow the WiFi setup (2.4GHz only, same as SmartPlug)

**Some sections of the strip aren't lighting up:**
- Check the connector clips between strip segments (if you cut/joined sections) — a loose clip is the most common cause of partial lighting
- Do not cut the strip except at the marked cut lines (indicated by scissor icons) — cutting elsewhere breaks the circuit

**Colors look different from the app preview:**
- Ensure the strip has a direct line of sight if using the included remote's IR receiver
- Update to the latest firmware via the GrowMart app, "Device Settings" → "Check for Updates" — early units shipped with a color-calibration firmware issue that was fixed via update

**Strip not responding to app or voice commands:**
- Confirm the strip shows as "Online" in the GrowMart app; if offline, follow the same WiFi troubleshooting steps as the SmartPlug (see "SmartPlug Won't Connect to WiFi")
- Power cycle by unplugging the adapter for 10 seconds

**Adhesive not sticking / strip falling off the wall:**
This is a common issue on textured or uneven surfaces. GrowMart includes mounting clips in the box as a backup — use these on surfaces where adhesive alone isn't sufficient. Replacement adhesive strips can be requested from support if the original adhesive has failed within the warranty period.
