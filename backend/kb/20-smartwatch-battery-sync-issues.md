# SmartWatch Fit Battery and Sync Issues

**Battery draining faster than expected (rated battery life: up to 7 days):**
- Always-on display mode significantly reduces battery life to 1–2 days — disable this in Settings if not needed
- Frequent notifications and continuous heart-rate monitoring also increase battery drain; adjust monitoring frequency in the GrowMart Fit app under "Health Settings"
- Extreme cold (below 5°C) temporarily reduces battery performance in most lithium batteries, including this one — this is expected and not a defect

**Watch not syncing data to the app:**
1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone and the watch is within range (under 10 meters)
2. Open the GrowMart Fit app and pull down on the home screen to force a manual sync
3. Check that the app has Bluetooth and location permissions enabled (required by Android for Bluetooth scanning)
4. Restart both the watch (hold the side button for 10 seconds) and your phone's Bluetooth connection

**Watch shows incorrect step count or heart rate:**
- Ensure the watch is worn snugly, about one finger-width above your wrist bone — a loose fit affects sensor accuracy
- Clean the sensor area on the back of the watch periodically with a dry cloth

**Watch won't turn on / black screen:**
1. Connect to the charging cable for at least 30 minutes, as a fully drained battery may not show charging indicators immediately
2. If still unresponsive after 30 minutes of charging, hold the side button for 15 seconds to force a restart

If the watch doesn't respond after a full charge and forced restart, this indicates a likely hardware issue — contact support with your Order ID for warranty assistance.
