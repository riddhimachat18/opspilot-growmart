# SmartPlug Won't Connect to WiFi

If your GrowMart SmartPlug (Single or Duo Pack) won't connect to WiFi during setup, work through these steps in order.

**Step 1: Check WiFi compatibility**
GrowMart SmartPlugs only support 2.4GHz WiFi networks, not 5GHz. If your router broadcasts a combined/single network name, you may need to temporarily create a separate 2.4GHz network in your router settings, or check your router manual for how to identify the 2.4GHz band.

**Step 2: Reset the SmartPlug**
Press and hold the power button on the SmartPlug for 8–10 seconds until the LED light rapidly blinks blue. This indicates it's in pairing mode and ready to reconnect.

**Step 3: Reconnect via the GrowMart app**
Open the GrowMart Home app → "Add Device" → select "SmartPlug" → follow the in-app WiFi setup, making sure your phone is connected to the same 2.4GHz network you want the plug to join.

**Step 4: Check router distance and interference**
Move the SmartPlug closer to your router temporarily during setup (within 5 meters), then reposition it after successful pairing. Thick walls, metal surfaces, and microwave ovens can interfere with the signal.

**Step 5: Restart your router**
If the above steps don't work, power cycle your router (unplug for 30 seconds) and retry setup.

**Still not working after all steps?** This may indicate a hardware fault. Contact support with the LED blink pattern you're seeing (steady blue, rapid blue, red, or off) so we can diagnose further or process a warranty replacement.
