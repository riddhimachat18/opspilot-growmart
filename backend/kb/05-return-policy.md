# Return Policy

GrowMart offers a 7-day return window for most products, starting from the date of delivery.

**Eligibility for return:**
- Item must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories, manuals, and tags intact
- Return request must be raised within 7 days of delivery
- Item must not be on the non-returnable list (see "Non-Returnable Items")

**How to initiate a return:**
1. Go to "My Orders" in your GrowMart account and select "Return Item," or contact support with your Order ID
2. Choose a reason for return (defective, not as described, changed mind, wrong item received)
3. A pickup will be scheduled within 2–3 business days at no extra cost for eligible returns
4. Once the item is received and inspected at our warehouse (typically 2–3 business days), your refund or exchange will be processed

**Exchanges:** If you'd prefer an exchange instead of a refund (e.g., different color or a different product of equal value), mention this when raising the return request. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

**Return shipping costs:** Free for defective, damaged, or wrongly shipped items. A ₹99 return shipping fee applies for "changed mind" returns, deducted from your refund amount.

For refund timelines once a return is approved, see "Refund Policy and Timelines."
