# Refund Policy and Timelines

Once a return is received and approved, or a refund is directly approved for an eligible issue (e.g., order cancellation, delivery failure), refunds are processed as follows:

**Refund method:** Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout (card, UPI, net banking, or wallet). GrowMart does not offer cash refunds for prepaid orders.

**Processing timelines:**
- Refund initiated by GrowMart: within 24 hours of approval
- Amount reflecting in your account: 5–7 business days for cards, 2–3 business days for UPI/wallets, depending on your bank's processing time

**Cash on Delivery (COD) orders:** Refunds for COD orders are issued via bank transfer. You'll be asked to share bank account details (account number, IFSC code) through a secure link after the return is approved.

**Partial refunds:** If only part of a multi-item order is returned, only the value of the returned item(s) is refunded; shipping charges are refunded only if the entire order is returned.

**Refund status:** You can check refund status under "My Orders" → select the order → "Refund Status," which shows Initiated / Processing / Completed.

**Refunds above ₹500:** Refunds above ₹500 for support-reported issues (not standard returns) require a brief manual review before approval to prevent fraud — this typically adds no more than 1 business day and does not affect standard return-based refunds.

If a refund hasn't reflected in your account 3 business days after the "Completed" status, contact support with your Order ID and bank/payment statement for verification.
