# Wireless Charger Not Fast Charging

If your GrowMart MagCharge 15W Wireless Charger seems to be charging slowly instead of at full 15W speed, check the following:

**1. Power adapter and cable**
The MagCharge requires a Quick Charge 3.0 or USB-PD compatible adapter rated at least 18W to deliver full 15W wireless output. Using a standard 5W phone adapter (common with older phones) will bottleneck charging speed significantly. Use the adapter and cable included in the box, or a certified fast-charging adapter.

**2. Phone case interference**
Fast wireless charging works best with cases under 3mm thick and made of plastic, silicone, or TPU. Cases with metal components, credit cards/cash stored in the back, or magnetic mounts can block or slow the charging coil connection. Try removing your case to see if charging speed improves.

**3. Alignment**
The charging coil must align with your phone's wireless charging coil (usually centered on the back of the phone). Misalignment by even 1–2 cm can drop charging speed or stop it entirely. The MagCharge has magnetic alignment guides — make sure you feel a slight magnetic "snap" when placing MagSafe-compatible phones.

**4. Device compatibility**
Not all phones support 15W wireless charging — many support only up to 7.5W or 10W via Qi standard. Check your phone's specifications; if it caps at a lower wattage, the charger is working correctly and the phone itself is limiting speed.

**5. Overheating protection**
If your phone or the charger feels warm, charging speed is automatically reduced as a safety measure. This is normal behavior, not a defect — speed resumes once temperature normalizes.

If you've confirmed compatible hardware and still see abnormally slow charging (e.g., under 5W with a compatible phone and adapter), this may indicate a defect — contact support for warranty assistance.
