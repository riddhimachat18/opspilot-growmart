# Damaged or Defective Item on Arrival

If your GrowMart order arrives damaged or a product doesn't work right out of the box (DOA — "dead on arrival"), this is handled separately from a standard return and does not count against your 7-day return window in the same way — report it as soon as possible for the fastest resolution.

**Steps to report:**
1. Do not discard the original packaging or accessories
2. Take clear photos of the damaged item, the packaging, and the shipping label
3. Contact GrowMart support within 48 hours of delivery with your Order ID and photos
4. Support will verify the issue and offer either a free replacement (subject to stock) or a full refund, your choice

**Priority handling:** Damaged/DOA reports are prioritized over standard returns — pickup and replacement dispatch are typically arranged within 24–48 hours of approval, faster than the standard return process.

**Free of charge:** There is no return shipping cost for damaged or defective items — GrowMart covers pickup in full.

If the product develops a fault after a period of normal use (not immediately on arrival), this is covered separately under warranty — see "Warranty Coverage" and "How to Claim Warranty."
