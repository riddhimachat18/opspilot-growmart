# EchoBuds Pro Pairing and Connection Issues

**First-time pairing:**
1. Place both earbuds in the charging case and keep the lid open
2. Press and hold the button on the case for 3 seconds until the LED blinks white
3. On your phone, go to Bluetooth settings and select "GrowMart EchoBuds Pro"
4. Once connected, a voice prompt will confirm "Connected"

**Only one earbud connects / audio only in one ear:**
1. Place both earbuds back in the case and close the lid for 10 seconds
2. Remove both earbuds together and check that both LEDs flash white briefly on removal
3. If only one connects, forget the device in your phone's Bluetooth settings, then reset both earbuds: place them in the case, press and hold the case button for 8 seconds until the LED blinks red-white alternately (factory reset), then re-pair from scratch

**Frequent disconnections or audio cutting out:**
- Ensure your phone's Bluetooth is updated and not connected to too many devices simultaneously (disconnect other Bluetooth accessories)
- Distance and obstructions matter — EchoBuds Pro has a rated range of ~10 meters with a clear line of sight; walls and pockets can reduce this
- Low battery in either earbud can cause instability — check charge level in the GrowMart app

**Earbuds not charging in the case:**
- Clean the charging pins on both the earbuds and inside the case with a dry cotton swab — dust/lint buildup is the most common cause
- Confirm the case itself is charged (LED indicator on the case front)

If pairing issues persist after a full factory reset, this may indicate a hardware fault — contact support for warranty assistance, especially if within 6 months of purchase.
