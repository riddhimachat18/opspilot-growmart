# Payment Methods and Payment Issues

**Accepted payment methods:**
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps)
- Net banking (all major Indian banks)
- Cash on Delivery (COD) — available for orders under ₹5,000 in most serviceable pin codes
- GrowMart Wallet / Store Credit, where applicable

**Payment failed but amount was debited:** This usually happens due to a delay between your bank confirming payment and it reflecting on GrowMart's system. In most cases, the amount is auto-reversed to your account within 5–7 business days by your bank. If your order does not show as confirmed and the amount isn't reversed within 7 business days, contact support with your bank statement and transaction reference number.

**Card declined at checkout:** Common causes include insufficient balance, incorrect CVV/OTP, your bank's international transaction block (if using the card abroad), or a temporary hold by your bank's fraud detection. Try an alternate payment method, or contact your bank if the issue persists across multiple attempts.

**COD not available at checkout:** COD eligibility depends on your pin code and order value. If COD isn't showing as an option, your order may exceed the COD limit or your pin code may not support COD — prepaid options remain available.

**Double payment / duplicate charge:** If you were charged twice for a single order, contact support immediately with both transaction references — duplicate charges are refunded within 3–5 business days after verification.
