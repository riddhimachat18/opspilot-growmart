# Shipping Policy

GrowMart ships to all serviceable pin codes across India. Orders are processed and dispatched from our Bengaluru and Delhi warehouses depending on stock availability and your delivery location.

**Shipping charges:**
- Orders above ₹999: free standard shipping
- Orders below ₹999: flat ₹79 shipping fee
- Express delivery (available in select metro cities): ₹149 additional, delivers in 1–2 business days

**Processing time:** Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation on business days (Monday–Saturday, excluding public holidays). Orders placed after 6 PM are processed the next business day.

**Serviceability:** Most pin codes across India are serviceable. If your pin code is not serviceable, this will be indicated at checkout before you complete payment. Remote or restricted areas (parts of the Northeast, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, and select rural pin codes) may have extended delivery timelines of 7–10 business days instead of the standard window.

**Packaging:** All electronics are shipped in tamper-evident packaging with a security seal. If a package arrives with a broken or tampered seal, do not accept the delivery and contact GrowMart support immediately with photos of the package.

For delivery timelines by city, see the "Delivery Timelines" article. For tracking your order, see "Order Tracking."
