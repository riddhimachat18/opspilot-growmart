# Account Login and Password Reset

**Resetting your password:**
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter the email or phone number linked to your account
3. You'll receive a reset link (email) or OTP (phone) valid for 15 minutes
4. Set a new password (minimum 8 characters, must include a number and a special character)

**Not receiving the OTP or reset email:**
- Check your spam/promotions folder for the email
- Ensure you're entering the exact email/phone number your account was registered with
- Wait at least 2 minutes before requesting another OTP — repeated rapid requests are temporarily rate-limited for security
- If still not received after 10 minutes, contact support to verify your account details

**Account locked after multiple failed attempts:** For security, accounts are temporarily locked for 30 minutes after 5 failed login attempts. Wait for the lockout to clear, or contact support to verify your identity and unlock it sooner.

**Changing your registered email or phone number:** Go to "My Account" → "Profile Settings." You'll need to verify the new email/phone via OTP before the change takes effect.

**Can't access your account at all (lost access to both email and phone):** Contact support with your Order ID history and any account details you can recall (name, past orders) so we can verify your identity manually and help restore access.
