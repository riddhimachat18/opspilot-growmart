# Warranty Coverage

GrowMart products come with a manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

**Warranty periods by product category:**
- Wireless chargers, power banks, cables & car chargers: 12 months
- Smart plugs and smart home devices: 12 months
- Wireless earbuds and Bluetooth speakers: 6 months
- Smartwatches: 12 months
- LED strip lights and accessories: 6 months

**What's covered:**
- Manufacturing defects (e.g., device fails to power on, charging circuit failure, non-functional buttons/sensors)
- Battery capacity dropping below 70% of original within the warranty period (for battery-powered devices)

**What's NOT covered:**
- Physical damage from drops, water exposure beyond the stated IP rating, or misuse
- Damage from using third-party chargers/accessories not recommended for the product
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., minor scuffs, faded printing)
- Software issues resolvable via a factory reset or firmware update
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers (not directly from GrowMart's official store or website)

**Warranty is void if:** the product has been opened, modified, or repaired by anyone other than GrowMart's authorized service center.

To claim a warranty repair or replacement, see "How to Claim Warranty."
