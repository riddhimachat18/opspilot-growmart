# Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene, safety, and stock-integrity reasons, the following categories are not eligible for return under GrowMart's standard 7-day return policy:

- **In-ear audio products** (earbuds, in-ear headphones) once the packaging seal is broken, unless defective
- **Items marked "Final Sale"** during clearance or flash sale events
- **Products with a removed or damaged serial number/warranty sticker**
- **Gift cards and promotional vouchers**
- **Custom or personalized items** (e.g., engraved products, if applicable)

**Important exception:** if a non-returnable item is defective, damaged on arrival, or significantly not as described, it remains eligible for a replacement or refund under warranty/defect provisions — the non-returnable list only applies to "changed my mind" style returns, not manufacturing defects.

If you're unsure whether your specific item qualifies, contact support with your Order ID and we'll confirm eligibility before you initiate a return, to save you the hassle of a rejected pickup.
