# GrowMart Plus Membership and Pricing Tiers

GrowMart offers a paid membership program, GrowMart Plus, alongside standard pay-per-order shopping.

**Standard (no membership):**
- Free shipping on orders above ₹999, flat ₹79 below that
- Standard 7-day return window
- Regular access to sale events

**GrowMart Plus — ₹299/year:**
- Free shipping on all orders, no minimum
- Extended 15-day return window instead of the standard 7 days
- Early access (24 hours) to sale events and new product launches
- 5% GrowMart Wallet cashback on every order, redeemable on future purchases
- Priority customer support queue (shorter wait times, dedicated support line)

**GrowMart Plus — ₹799/2 years:**
- All Plus benefits above, at a discounted 2-year rate (saves ~₹200 vs two 1-year memberships)

**How to subscribe:** "My Account" → "GrowMart Plus" → choose a plan → complete payment. Membership activates immediately and benefits apply to your very next order.

**Cancellation:** GrowMart Plus can be cancelled anytime from "My Account" → "GrowMart Plus" → "Manage Membership." Since it's a prepaid annual/biennial plan, cancellation stops auto-renewal but does not refund the remaining active period, except within the first 7 days of initial signup (full refund available if unused).

**Bulk/corporate pricing** is handled separately — see "Bulk and Corporate Orders."
