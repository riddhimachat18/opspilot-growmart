# Delivery Timelines by Location

Estimated delivery timelines depend on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout.

**Metro cities (Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata):**
- Standard shipping: 2–3 business days
- Express shipping: 1–2 business days

**Tier 2 cities and towns:**
- Standard shipping: 4–6 business days
- Express shipping: not currently available

**Remote or rural pin codes:**
- Standard shipping only: 7–10 business days

These timelines begin from the date of dispatch, not the date of order placement. You will receive a dispatch confirmation email/SMS with a tracking link once your order leaves our warehouse.

**Factors that may extend delivery:**
- Public holidays and regional festivals
- Extreme weather disruptions
- High-demand periods (sale events, festive season)
- Incomplete or incorrect address details provided at checkout

If your order has exceeded the estimated delivery window by more than 2 business days, refer to the "Delayed or Lost Order" article for next steps.
