# How to Track Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS to the contact details provided at checkout.

**To track your order:**
1. Click the tracking link in your dispatch confirmation email, or
2. Log in to your GrowMart account and go to "My Orders" → select the order → "Track Package"

**Order status definitions:**
- **Order Confirmed** — payment received, order is being prepared
- **Processing** — item is being picked and packed at the warehouse
- **Dispatched** — order has left the warehouse and is with the courier partner
- **Out for Delivery** — package is with the local delivery agent and expected same day
- **Delivered** — package has been handed over to the recipient

Tracking information typically updates within 24 hours of a status change but may occasionally lag behind the courier partner's actual status, especially over weekends.

If tracking shows no movement for more than 3 business days, or shows "Delivered" but you haven't received the package, contact GrowMart support with your order ID so we can raise an investigation with the courier partner.
