# How to Factory Reset Your SmartPlug

A factory reset is useful if you're giving the device to someone else, moving it to a new WiFi network, or troubleshooting persistent connection issues.

**To factory reset:**
1. Ensure the SmartPlug is plugged into a power outlet
2. Press and hold the power button for 10–12 seconds, until the LED light blinks rapidly (blue) three times and then turns off briefly
3. Release the button — the LED should now blink slowly, indicating the reset is complete and the device is back to factory settings
4. Set up the SmartPlug again as a new device through the GrowMart Home app

**Note:** Factory resetting removes the device from your account and any automation/schedules you had set up for it. You'll need to recreate schedules after re-adding the device.

**If the reset doesn't work (LED doesn't respond):** Unplug the SmartPlug from the outlet, wait 10 seconds, plug it back in, and retry the reset steps immediately after power-up. If it still doesn't respond, this likely indicates a hardware issue — contact support for warranty assistance.
